About Bhawandas and Sons

Bhawandas and Sons has proudly served the Sardarshahr community for over 6 years. What began as a humble family-run shop has now grown into a trusted destination for high-quality clothing and tailoring services. Known for our commitment to customer satisfaction, we offer an extensive range of products including raw cloth, designer sarees, ethnic wear, blazers, kurtas, and accessories. Whether you're shopping for everyday essentials or preparing for a special occasion, Bhawandas and Sons has something for everyone.

Our expert tailors bring decades of craftsmanship to every stitch, ensuring each garment fits perfectly and reflects your style. We also stay updated with the latest fashion trends to bring modern styles to our local community without compromising on comfort and quality.

Visit our welcoming store in the heart of Sardarshahr to experience personalized service, affordable prices, and a wide selection of apparel that suits all ages and tastes.
